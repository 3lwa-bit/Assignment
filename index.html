Now, let's talk about the five most famous countries for food.🎀

Egypt 🌄

Koshari is one of my favorite Egyptian dishes! It’s a delicious mix of rice, pasta, lentils, and spicy tomato sauce — topped with crispy onions. Many tourists visit Egypt just to try authentic Koshari from the local street restaurants. It’s not just food; it’s a true taste of Egyptian culture and tradition!

Italy 🍕

Margherita Pizza is one of Italy’s most loved dishes. It’s simple but full of flavor — made with fresh tomatoes, mozzarella cheese, and basil. This classic pizza perfectly represents the colors of the Italian flag and the taste of true Italian tradition.

France 🥐

The croissant is one of the most famous French pastries. It’s soft, buttery, and perfectly golden — best enjoyed fresh in the morning with a cup of coffee. In Paris, bakeries fill the air with the smell of freshly baked croissants, making breakfast a truly special experience.
